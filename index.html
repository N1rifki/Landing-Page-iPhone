iPhone 15

Titanium. So Strong. So light. Powerful.

The iPhone 15 Titanium is a premium model from Apple that uses aerospace-grade titanium, making it stronger, lighter, and more durable. With an elegant and lightweight design, it features a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display that supports Dolby Vision and ProMotion 120Hz. Performance is powered by the fast and efficient A17 Pro chip, along with an improved main camera for high-quality photos. The battery lasts longer and supports fast charging via USB-C and MagSafe. The iPhone 15 Titanium also comes with iOS 17, IP68 water and dust resistance, and several premium color options.
